Key Applications

Automotive & Electric Vehicles

Lightweight structural panels, battery enclosures, and aerodynamic components that improve efficiency and range for electric vehicles. Our composites reduce weight by up to 40% compared to traditional materials.

Wind & Renewable Energy

Long-lasting, low-weight blades and housings for wind turbines and solar structures. Our materials withstand harsh environmental conditions while reducing maintenance costs by 30%.

Aerospace & UAVs

Components for drones, aircraft interiors, and structural fuselage elements that meet stringent aerospace standards while reducing fuel consumption.

Sports & Lifestyle Equipment

Performance-enhancing materials for bicycles, racquets, helmets, and outdoor gear used by elite athletes worldwide.
